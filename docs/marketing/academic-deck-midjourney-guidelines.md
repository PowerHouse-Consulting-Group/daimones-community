# MidJourney Guidelines — Academic & Institutions Presentation Deck

**Purpose:** Generate slide BACKGROUND IMAGES for B2B institutional outreach deck  
**⚠️ IMPORTANT:** This file generates background images ONLY. For slide text content (headlines, body, bullet points, speaker notes), see **`academic-deck-slide-content.md`** in this same directory.  
**Brand Aesthetic:** Classical Scholarly — matte stone, gold leaf, warm natural light, museum exhibition quality  
**Tone:** Scholarly, rigorous, authoritative (NOT marketing-speak, NOT startup hype)  
**Visual Reference:** Oxford University Press, museum exhibition catalogs, university press publications — NOT cyberpunk, NOT crypto, NOT tech startup

---

## Design Philosophy

This deck targets **university department heads, philosophy professors, and institutional procurement committees** (ages 45–70). The visual language must signal:

- **Academic prestige** — think Bodleian Library, not Blade Runner
- **Institutional trust** — think university press, not Discord server
- **Scholarly gravitas** — think peer-reviewed journal, not pitch deck

The ancient-meets-modern concept stays. The execution shifts from cyberpunk to **classical scholarly**: warm golden light replaces neon, manuscripts replace holograms, natural shadows replace LED glow.

## Usage Notes

1. **No text in images** — MJ hallucinates Greek characters. Add text in post (Keynote/PowerPoint/Figma)
2. **Negative space** — All prompts include composition guidance for text overlay
3. **Consistency** — Use same --v 7 --style raw parameters across all slides
4. **Variations** — Generate 4 variations per slide, select best composition
5. **Aspect ratio** --ar 16:9 matches standard presentation format
6. **Light/Dark Mix** — Consider alternating dark and warm ivory backgrounds for visual rhythm (e.g., Slides 1,3,5,7,9 dark; Slides 2,4,6,8,10 warm ivory)

## B2B vs B2C Visual Identity

| Dimension | B2B Academic (THIS DECK) | B2C Growth (Web/Social) |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Audience** | Professors, department heads, procurement | Individual users, tech-savvy philosophers |
| **Visual language** | Museum exhibition, university press | Ancient temple × cyberpunk, manuscript cryptic |
| **Color palette** | Warm gold, ivory, charcoal, bronze | Dark navy, neon accents, matte black |
| **Lighting** | Golden hour, natural light, warm tungsten | Dramatic neon, atmospheric fog, cinematic |
| **Texture** | Matte stone, aged parchment, marble | Circuit boards, chrome, glass |
| **Signal** | "We belong in your institution" | "We think differently from corporate AI" |
