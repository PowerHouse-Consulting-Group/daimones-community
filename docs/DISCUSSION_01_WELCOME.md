## 🎯 What is daïmōnes?

daïmōnes is an AI-powered philosophical dialogue platform that brings Aristotle's wisdom to the digital age. Engage in deep philosophical inquiry using:

- **Polytonic Ancient Greek** (authentic orthography with proper accents and breathings)
- **Modern Greek**
- **English**

Ask Aristotle questions about philosophy, ethics, logic, metaphysics, politics, and more. Receive responses in authentic Polytonic Greek with English translations, following Aristotelian methodology and sources from the Corpus Aristotelicum.
